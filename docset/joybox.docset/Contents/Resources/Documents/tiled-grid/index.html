ACTIONS & ANIMATIONS

TiledGrid

The TiledGrid class . . .

fade_out_bottom_left_tiles

CCFadeOutBLTiles action. Fades out the tiles in a Bottom-Left direction:

fade_out_bottom_left_tiles_action = TiledGrid. fade_out_bottom_left_tiles grid:[100, 200]

Options

duration: - Float - Default: 0.3
Sets the total duration time of the Action.

jump_tiles_3D

CCJumpTiles3D action. A sin function is executed to move the tiles across the Z axis:

jump_tiles_3D_action = TiledGrid.jump_tiles_3D grid:[100, 200]

Options

jumps - Integer - Default: 0
Sets the total of jumps.
amplitude - Float - Default: 0.0
Sets the amplitude of the jumps.
duration: - Float - Default: 0.3
Sets the total duration time of the Action.
